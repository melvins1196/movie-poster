Squirrels Attack

A nut crisis emerges in the squirrel community. The squirrels running out of nuts are now beginning to starve. As start to reach their limit their hunger turns towards humans. Millions of squirrels flood into the city of nutvile to take everyones nuts. The squirrels going bananas have no mercy towards the humans. Every kitchen in Nutvile are being attacked for their nuts. But one family will stop at nothing to protect their nuts as they face the squirrels head on.
