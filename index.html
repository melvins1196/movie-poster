Squirrels Attack

A nut crisis emerges in the squirrel community. The squirrels are running out of nuts are now beginning to starve. As they start to reach their limit, their hunger turns towards humans. Millions of squirrels flood into the city of Nutville to take everyones nuts. The squirrels are going bananas and have no mercy towards the humans. Every kitchen in Nutville is being attacked for their nuts. But one family will stop at nothing to protect their nuts as they face the squirrels head on.
